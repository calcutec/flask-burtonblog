What is it?

This Great Pic is a website and app for sharing your very best photographic creations.

It has been designed to meet the requirements of serious photographers wishing to share and get feedback on their work and technique. This Great Pic stores and displays a photo in its original aspect-ratio and in a resolution optimized for the device and screen on which it is being viewed.

It also retains and gives automatic access to the photo file's exif data. Most often stripped from a photo file by media servers to reduce its size, this meta-data contains critical information on when, how (in terms of aperture, speed, light-settings, etc) and with what type of camera and lens the photo was taken.

More...

A file chosen for upload must be an original version with exif data intact. This helps to:
  • 1) Ensure that users submit photos they have taken themselves and presumably own.
  • 2) Determine the best size for viewing the photo on either a desktop computer or mobile device.
  • 3) Allow users to share info about the camera, lens, aperture, timing, light-setting, etc, used in capturing a given photo.

How do I join?

Click the arrow at the top right to go to the signup and login page. New users can join either by one-click login connected to their Facebook or Google accounts, or by providing their email and a password and user name.

This Great Pic will never share your photos, email, comment's content or other user information with companies or other groups.

How does it work?

Pretty much like most social-media apps and sites: click appropriately-labeled buttons or icons to view, share, edit, delete, or comment on photos.

Buttons for filtering content and navigating the site are accessed through the menu on the top left of each page.
