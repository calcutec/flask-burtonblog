What is it?

This Great Pic is a website and app for sharing your very best photographic creations. The aim is that members will select the greatest examples from a given shoot or collection of photos for sharing and discussion. Intended as a site for amateur and professional photographers to share and get feedback on their work and technique, This Great Pic stores and displays photos in their original aspect-ratio and in a resolution true to the original yet optimized for the specific device and screen on which it is being viewed. For archiving purposes as well as for discussions, it also retains and gives automatic access to a digital photo file's exif data and the important information it contains regarding how (in terms of f-stop, speed, light-settings, etc) and with what type of camera and lens a photo was taken.

The Full Wonky

Related to the above, a file chosen for upload must be an original version with exif data intact. This helps with:
  • 1) Ensuring that users submit photos they have taken themselves and presumably own
  • 2) Determing the best size for viewing the photo on either a desktop computer or mobile device
  • 3) Allowing users to share info about the camera, lens, aperture, timing, light-setting, etc, used in capturing a given photo

How do I join?

Click the arrow at the top righ to go to the signup and login page. New users can join either by one-click login connected to their Facebook or Google accounts, or by providing their email and a password and user name. This Great Pic does not and will not ever share your photos, email, discussion content or other information with other companies or groups.

How does it work?

Pretty much like most social-media sites: you click appropriately-labeled buttons to view, share, edit, delete, filter or comment on photos. To view an image in full-screen mode, double-click it, or double-tap it if on a mobile device. This also opens up a gallery view, in which you can scroll or swipe left and right to see other photos in that group.
